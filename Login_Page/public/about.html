About E-Muse

E-Muse is a prototype media-player that uses Emotions and Biometrics to play and choose songs! It is currently under development
E-muse uses a host of technologies such as CNN, NodeJS,MongoDB,ExpressJS among many others.

The name E-Muse came from the Greek Goddesses of Literature, Science and the arts (called Muses).
